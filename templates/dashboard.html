ABOUT US

Originating from Taiwan, CHICHA San Chen (吃茶三千) was founded in 1998 with a mission to make high-quality, freshly brewed teas accessible to discerning tea drinkers. Built on a sense of mindfulness with every sip, CHICHA San Chen (吃茶三千) is handcrafted by Taiwanese tea masters with over 20 years of experience, including having its own tea mountain, professional tea tasters, and tea roasters.

OUR TEA

CHICHA San Chen’s (吃茶三千) Green Tea holds a prestigious Highest International Authority ITI certification for quality and utilises an exclusive patented product and technology that allows more than 1,000 custom settings and a three-stage temperature control brewing technique to bring out the tea’s best flavour and natural scent, maintaining consistency in the quality and taste profiles of their bubble tea.



OUR SUSTAINABILITY WORK

What we do to protect environment
Brew the Perfect Tea

We offer refreshing drinks made with premium tea leaves, allowing customers to choose different tea bases and sugar levels to promote a healthy lifestyle.

Small Steps, Big Changes

With a streamlined online ordering system, we increase staff efficiency and enhance the customer experience. Plus, every purchase earns you points through our Sip & Earn program, helping you enjoy your favorite drinks while contributing to a greener future.

Customize and Sustain

We allow customers to customize their drink portions, helping to reduce food waste. Additionally, we strongly encourage the use of biodegradable straws.
